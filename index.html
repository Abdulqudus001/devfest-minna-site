What is DevFest?

DevFests are community-led developer events hosted by Google Developer Groups(GDGs) around the globe. These events cover sessions on multiple product such as Android, Firebase, Google Cloud Platform, Google Assistant, Flutter, machine learning with TensorFlow, and mobile web.

DevFest Abuja is organized by the GDGs in North Central Nigeria (GDG Abuja, GDG Keffi, GDG Lafia, GDG Ilorin GDG Makurdi and GDG Minna). Wanna find out about Devfest happening in other cities near you? Check out https://devfest.withgoogle.com

What is GDG?

Google Developers Group (GDG) - is open and volunteer geek community, run by passionate individuals in local developer communities with the aim of sharing ideas and network with fellow developers and is open to and free for all interested.
